Chapter 1 +

wa is a particle which follows a noun, singling out the topic of the sentence. For example

  • Gurei-san wa bengoshi desu
  • Watashi wa Nihon-jin desu

ka is a question particle. Add it at the end of the sentence to make it a question. No other change to the sentence may be needed. For example

  • Gurei-san wa bengoshi desu ka
  • Watashi wa Nihon-jin desu ka

Ja/dewa arimasen is the negative form of desu. For example

  • Gurei-san wa gakusei desu ka
  • Iie, gakusei dewa arimasen

No follows a noun and expresses possession or affiliation. When we introduce ourselves we usually say Hajimemashite. Company no Anton.

Kochira wa is a polite way of introducing someone. Such as Kochira wa W3 Schools no Tama desu.

When saying a phone number aloud, put a no for each -. For example 03-7462-9164 is pronounced as zero san no nana yon roku ni no kyu ichi roku yon
